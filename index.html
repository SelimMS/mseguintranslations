About Me

I'm Marjorie Seguin and I provide English to French translation services.
I take great pride in my work and since I started my translation career in 2005 my aim has always been the same: producing the best translations I possibly can.
As a French native speaker with over 10 years experience in the translation industry I have the necessary experience and know-how to produce quality translations that will benefit your business.

So if you have a written document that needs translating into French and quality is required, I can help you.
My areas of expertise are marketing, business and sports & fitness.
I also specialise in audiovisual translation and provide a subtitling service.

Why trust me?

I am a fully qualified translator with an MA in Bilingual Translation (English-French) obtained at the University of Westminster in 2005.

Moreover, I abide by a strict code of conduct to ensure the highest standards possible, and as such I only translate into my mother tongue and only accept projects within my areas of expertise.
